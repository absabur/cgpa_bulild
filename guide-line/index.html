How to use?

Step 1: Select department

Step 2: You can also search for a department, It will be highlighted.

Step 3: Select grade of your course. You will get here all of the years.

Step 4: If there any multiple choice course, It will be disable autometically.

Step 5: You can see the CGPA instantly.

Step 6: From the save button you can get marksheet.

Step 7: Write your details, if you want this in marksheet.

Step 8: You can save the marksheet eiter pdf or image.

Step 9: In the bottom of the page, you will get overall CGPA.

Step 10: You can also downlaod overall marksheet from there.
