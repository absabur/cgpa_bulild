Welcome to our fully automatic website designed to simplify CGPA calculation for any department of the National University. With our tool, you can effortlessly calculate your CGPA by selecting your department and grades for each course.

How It Works

  1. 1. Choose Your Department: Select your department from the provided options.
  2. 2. Select Your Grades: Click on the grades for each subject. The subjects and their respective credits are preloaded for your convenience.
  3. 3. Get Your CGPA: Instantly receive your CGPA by selecting grade.
  4. 4. Download Detailed Marksheet: Download a comprehensive marksheet with full details for your records.

Why Use Our CGPA Calculator?

  1. * User-Friendly Interface: Designed to be intuitive and easy to use.
  2. * Preloaded Data: Subjects and credits are already given, saving you time.
  3. * Instant Results: Calculate your CGPA in seconds.
  4. * Detailed Marksheet: Downloadable for personal use or academic purposes.
